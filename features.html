FEATURES

Built entirely around how schools manage inventory

From supply closets to lab equipment, TBD handles every corner of your school's inventory ecosystem.

Real-time inventory visibility

See what you have, where you have it, and who has it—instantly. Track inventory by classroom, department, or location without waiting for syncs.

  • Live stock levels across all locations
  • Complete audit trail of every transaction
  • Filter by department, teacher, or category
  • Search with barcode or QR code

Classroom & club management

Assign inventory to specific classrooms, school clubs, and elective programs. Teachers can request items, and admins approve or fulfill in seconds.

Smart alerts & notifications

Get notified before supplies run out. Set thresholds for each item. Teachers get reminders before class; admins see reorder suggestions.

Barcode & QR support

Label supplies with barcodes or QR codes for instant check-in/out. Works on any device—Chromebook, iPad, phone. No special software needed.

Role-based access

Teachers see their classroom supplies. Admins see everything. Set custom permissions: view-only, request-only, or full control per user.

Budget tracking

Track spending per department or location. Set annual budgets and see real-time spend. Forecast next year's needs based on historical data.

Reporting & analytics

Export inventory reports. Analyze usage trends over time. Identify slow-moving stock and cost-saving opportunities.
