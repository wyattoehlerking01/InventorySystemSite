Pricing questions

Is there a setup fee?

No. Just pick a plan and start your free trial immediately. Setup and onboarding are included.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

What if I exceed my location limit?

If you add more locations, we'll notify you. You can upgrade your plan, or we can discuss a custom arrangement.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes. Pay yearly and save about 15%. You'll also get priority support and early access to new features.

Is there a free version?

No free tier, but all plans include a 30-day free trial with full feature access—no credit card required.
