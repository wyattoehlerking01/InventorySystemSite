Demo questions

How long is the demo?

Typically 30-45 minutes, depending on your questions. We can focus on specific areas like your department or use case.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No prep needed. But if you have specific questions or challenges, sharing them beforehand helps us tailor the demo.

Can I invite other team members?

Absolutely. We recommend having teachers, admins, and IT leadership on the call for different perspectives.

No problem. We'll work with you to find a time that works. Reschedule anytime via email.
