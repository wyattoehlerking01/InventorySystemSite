ABOUT US

Building the future of school inventory

We started TBD because we saw schools struggling with spreadsheets and outdated systems. Now we're changing that.

Our mission

Empower educators and administrators to spend less time managing inventory and more time focused on what matters: teaching and learning.

We believe every school deserves modern tools, regardless of budget. That's why TBD is built specifically for schools, from the ground up.

Why we exist

We talked to teachers, supply coordinators, and admins. We heard the same story: hours lost to inventory chaos. Duplicated purchases. Missed supplies.

TBD exists to solve that. To bring order, save time, and let schools focus on education.

Our values

School-first

Every feature is designed by talking to real educators. Not a generic inventory tool—built for schools.

Simple

Powerful features that are easy to use. Teachers shouldn't need training. Systems should work for them.

Transparent

No surprise fees. No artificial per-seat limits. Honest pricing, honest product development.

Reliable

Your school depends on TBD. We take uptime, security, and privacy seriously.
