INTEGRATIONS

Works with tools your school already uses

TBD integrates with the platforms educators rely on daily. Import rosters, sync classrooms, and automate workflows.

Featured integrations

Google Classroom

Native

Sync classroom rosters and creation dates. Teachers can quickly assign inventory to their active classes. Deprovisioning is automatic.

  • Auto-sync rosters
  • Class-level permissions
  • Automatic deprovisioning

Clever

Native

Import student and staff rosters from Clever's roster sync. TBD automatically updates as users are added or removed.

  • Annual roster sync
  • Staff directory import
  • Single sign-on (SSO)

Microsoft Teams

Native

Notify teams of low stock alerts. Create TBD requests channels for requesting supplies. Get real-time updates without leaving Teams.

  • Alerts & notifications
  • Request management
  • Team-level tracking

Google Drive

Native

Export reports and inventory lists to Google Drive. Share inventory snapshots with staff. Collaborate on supply planning in real-time.

  • Report export
  • Real-time sharing
  • Collaborative editing

  • Course roster sync

  • Grade-level tracking
